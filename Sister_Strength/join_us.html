MEMBERSHIP REQUIREMENTS

Sister Strength has an open door policy. We are here to support and encourage spiritual development, not to be exclusive or judgemental. We have one golden rule at our meetings, what is discussed at the meeting, stays at the meeting. Members are responsible for protecting each Sister's privacy. Our goal is to create a safe atmosphere for discussing life issues, personal goals, and spiritual growth in a secure, confidential environment.

  • Come with an open mind and an open heart
  • Always begin by listening from the heart
  • Be prayerful and led by the spirit in your response
