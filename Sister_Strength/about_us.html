SISTER STRENGTH MISSION STATEMENT

Sister Strength exists to support women of all ages and to be united in love. (Colossians 2:2) To realize that they aren't alone when dealing with various life changing issues. (Romans 15:1, 20)
We will strive to accomplish this by:

  • Developing a support system; offering encouragement to one another. (Hebrews 10: 24-25)
  • Help one another try to overcome strongholds.
  • Encouraging others to trust the Lord.
  • To grow in faith.
  • Obtain a positive image of themselves.
  • Develop a personal relationship with the Lord through prayer, studying the word and fasting.

SISTER STRENGTH CREED

We are all sisters, we are one. No matter who we are or where we come from we all share the love of Christ.

Together we praise him worship, and adore him together.
Together we study his word.
Together we laugh, and together we cry.
Together we rejoice with one another in victory.
Together we uplift and encourage one another through trials
and tribulations, through the good and the bad in our lives.
Together we cast our burdens to the Lord.
Together we walk in faith.
Together we fast and pray for deliverance.
Together we remind one another that God is in control in our lives.
We trust God for all things in our lives.

While growing in power, for the love of God in me, we are sisters standing together in unity and blessing my sisters.

ROMONA A. WILLIAMS - President/Founder

For the past 5 years as the leader of Sister Strength, she has provided a safe therapeutic haven for women to be able to come to fellowship, praise, worship, study the word of God with the intent to inspire, restore and heal. Sister Strength's sessions provide women of all ages with the opportunity to share life issues, challenges, testimonies and blessings to encourage the faith, endurance and love.

Over the last 20 years, Romona has been a member and leader of various women's ministries. She is a member of St. Peter C.M.E. Church in Kansas City, Kansas.

  • Women's Sunday School Teacher
  • Youth Ministry leader from 1999-2005
  • 2008 Alumni member and Group Leader of "Purpose and Destiny"
  • "Women of Faith" Member
  • Bible Study Class Facilitator

Professionally, Mrs. Williams served various roles at Hallmark Cards prior to her retirement in 2007, after 38 years of employment. She currently serves on as a Board Member of the Kansas City, Kansas Women's Chamber of Commerce.

Mrs. Williams life mission is inspired by Colossians 2:2-3 (NIV):
"My purpose is that they may be encouraged in heart and united in love, so that they may have the full riches of complete understanding, in order that they may know the mystery of God, namely, Christ, in whom are hidden all the treasures of wisdom and knowledge."
